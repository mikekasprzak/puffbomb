SDL Notes
------------
To disable the stdout.txt stderr.txt redirection, configure SDL like so:

./configure --disable-stdio-redirect


Libraries included:

-Lmingw32/lib -lmingw32 -lSDLmain -lSDL -lSDL_image \
-lkernel32 -luser32 -lgdi32 -lcomdlg32 -lole32 -ldinput \
-lddraw -ldxguid -lwinmm -ldsound -lopengl32 -lglu32 \
-mwindows
